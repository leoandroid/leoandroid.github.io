[TeXt](https://kitian616.github.io/jekyll-TeXt-theme/docs/zh/layouts) is a super customizable Jekyll theme for personal site, team site, blog, project, documentation, etc. Similar to iOS 11 style, it has large and prominent titles, round buttons and cards.


## Skins

TeXt has 6 built-in skins, you can also set up your own skin.

| `default` | `dark` | `forest` |
